Fault-Tolerant Quadruped Locomotion

Reinforcement Learning Policies for Robust Robot Movement

About This Project

This project showcases advanced reinforcement learning policies designed for fault-tolerant locomotion in quadruped robots. Our research focuses on developing robust movement strategies that enable robots to continue functioning effectively even when facing mechanical failures or environmental challenges.
